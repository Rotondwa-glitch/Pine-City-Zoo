PANDA

Pandas, also known as giant pandas, are native to the mountainous regions of central China. They're instantly recognizable with their iconic black-and-white fur and their obsession with bamboo.

Despite being classified as carnivores, pandas are almost entirely herbivorous — bamboo makes up about 99% of their diet! They use their strong jaws and pseudo-thumbs (an extended wrist bone) to grasp and munch bamboo stalks with ease.

Pandas are generally solitary animals and spend most of their day eating and sleeping. They can eat up to 14 hours a day and snooze the rest away. Talk about living the dream!

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM
